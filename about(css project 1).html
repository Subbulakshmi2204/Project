About

Our Flip Card Memory Game is a brain-training adventure that involves matching signs and pictograms to solve puzzles. The objective is to beat the clock and pair up the cards before time runs out. It can be customized to meet specific learning objectives by replacing signs and pictograms with images or text, adjusting game instructions and objectives, and creating themed challenges.

How to Play

  • The game consists of an even number of tiles with images on one side and a generic back. Each image appears on precisely two tiles.
  • When the game starts, all tiles are turned face down.
  • The player flips over two cards by clicking on them. If they match, they remain face up; otherwise, they flip back after a short delay.
  • The goal is to find all the matching image pairs in the least number of tries.
